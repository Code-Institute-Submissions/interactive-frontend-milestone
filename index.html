1. Burj Khalifa

About:

At 2,711 feet, the Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world, and the UAE’s most iconic building. Visitors can head to At The Top, an outdoor observation deck on the 124th floor, to check out the view. Up on the 148th floor is the SKY Lounge, which is one of the world’s highest observation decks.
